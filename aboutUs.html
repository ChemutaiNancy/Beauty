About City Parlour

We are City Beauty Parlour located in Westlands Nairobi, Kenya. We have been offering the best in beauty services for more than 5 years to both ladies and gentlemen. The beauty parlour offers complete massage, hair, nail and foot care services that include hair cuts, waxing, massage, manicure, pedicure, fills and many more. Every customer is handled by a well trained techies with professional certification to work in the beauty industry.
